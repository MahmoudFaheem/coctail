The COCTAIL Repository

The COVID-19 Clinical Trial And Intervention Literature Hub, known as COCTAIL, is an active database comprising an evidence table that includes papers extracted from PubMed. These papers report on clinical and epidemiological studies, including clinical trials, case reports, or meta-analyses, related to the COVID-19 pandemic.
